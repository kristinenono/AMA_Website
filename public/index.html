Welcome to AMA UW-Madison

About us

AMA is a professional association for students interested in leading the practice, teaching and development of marketing knowledge worldwide. Our meetings are filled with speakers from top companies including P&G, Footlocker, Sargento, and more. These professionals come to discuss the industry and their experiences within the field of marketing.
